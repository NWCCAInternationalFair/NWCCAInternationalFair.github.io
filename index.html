About

We are a group of students who take pride in showing off different cultures and how they differ from the cultures we grew up in. We want to inform people about the differences in an educational way, making sure that they understand that stereotypes normally are not a factual depiction of many cultures. When we set-up this event we want every one to feel welcomed and embrace their cultures or ones they connect with.

We do the international fair so that the whole school can have a chance to learn about the world. Students can try new foods, receive an overview about other countries, see examples of habits, traditionl wear, and have a hands-on experience with the culture. At the international fair we set up booths that represent regions of the world. At our booths we present information, set up different activities, provide traditonal foods, and teach traditonal dances and games at some booths.

We hope that everyone who comes to our fair, will leave a bit more open-minded and informed than when they got there. Taking the following survey will help us plan future International fairs to come.
